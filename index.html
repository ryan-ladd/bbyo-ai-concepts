Program Planning Partner

An AI-powered digital workspace that guides teens through every step of planning and running a BBYO program.

The Problem

Teens have the ideas but struggle to turn them into polished programs. Staff are stretched thin coaching dozens of teens through a multi-step planning process with no digital support.

The Solution

A guided digital form powered by AI that helps generate, refine, and check every piece of a program — from brainstorming to the final PDF and staff sign-off.

Key Features
💡AI Brainstorm with Toolbox access
✍️Title & description elevations
🔗Branded short link generator
🎨Canva AI flyer integration
📋Program outline & script generator
🏠Hosting & environment suggestions
💰Budget builder & supply list
✅Pluralism & insurance compliance check
📄One-click PDF program generation
📬Staff email delivery

02

Calendar Coordinator

An intelligent calendaring tool that aggregates all scheduling layers to generate the optimal chapter semester calendar.

The Problem

Finding the right date is one of the most time-consuming and overlooked parts of chapter planning. Holidays, school schedules, testing, and community conflicts can sink a program before it starts.

The Solution

Feed in local school calendars, Jewish holidays, regional BBYO dates, and board member schedules — and the Calendar Coordinator generates a strategically optimized semester calendar with one click.

Key Features
📅School & local holiday calendar upload
✡️Built-in Jewish holiday awareness
🗺️Regional blackout date blocking
👤Personal board member schedule input
🎯Funnel-based event prioritization
🔄Interactive drag-and-drop calendar
🎨Canva AI branded calendar export
💭Per-event program ideation prompts

03

Ask Anita

A friendly, knowledgeable AI chatbot trained across all of BBYO's digital content — ready to help anyone find what they need, fast.

The Problem

With 200+ pages of content on the new site — plus resources, articles, and off-platform docs — teens, parents, advisors, and even staff struggle to find information quickly. Navigation and search alone aren't enough.

The Solution

Ask Anita is an always-on AI assistant embedded across the BBYO digital ecosystem. Trained on all BBYO content, she can answer questions, surface the right resources, and connect people to the right opportunities in seconds.

Key Use Cases
📍BBYO Near Me — events, chapters, contacts
☀️Summer program matching by preference
📖Policy, travel, and deadline FAQs
🔗Get Involved — role & opportunity matching
💻myBBYO troubleshooting support
💰Scholarship info and deadlines

At a Glance

Three concepts, different audiences, and different levels of investment.

Concept 01

Program Planning Partner

End-to-end AI workspace guiding chapter teens from brainstorm to PDF — deeply integrated with BBYO's content and Canva.

High Effort — 9/10
Concept 02

Calendar Coordinator

Intelligent semester calendar builder that layers school, religious, regional, and personal schedules into one optimized view.

Medium-High Effort — 7/10
Concept 03

Ask Anita

A friendly AI chatbot trained across all of BBYO's digital content — helping any audience find what they need in seconds.

Medium Effort — 5/10
Recommendation

Ask Anita is the strongest candidate for a first build — lower risk, broadly useful across all audiences, and demonstrably valuable from day one. Calendar Coordinator solves a concrete, recurring pain point for chapter boards and staff. Program Planning Partner has the highest ceiling but warrants a phased approach, starting with the brainstorm and outline tools before tackling the full integration suite.
